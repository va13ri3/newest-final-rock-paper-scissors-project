Game Rules
  • 👊 Rock beats Scissors
  • ✋ Paper beats Rock

  • 🤝 Identical choices result in a tie.
Scoring

Win a round to get 1 point. Ties and losses do not add points to your score.

Resetting

Click the "New Game" button at any time to clear the scores and start over.
